Challenges Kenyan athletes face

1.Financial Hardship:Many Kenyan athletes come from impoverished backgrounds and struggle to afford basic necessities like proper nutrition, training equipment, and travel to competitions. 2.Infrastructure and Training Facilities:Many athletes lack access to proper sports science services like physiotherapy, sports nutrition,and psychological support. 3.Political Uncertainty and Infighting:Political uncertainty anddisputes within sporting federations can disrupt the development and management of sports. 4.Gender Inequality:Inadequate sports infrastructure disproportionately affects women's athletic development and performance. 5.Mental Health:Kenyan athletes face immense pressure to succeed and provide for their families, leading to mental health challenges. 6. Lack of Support and Exploitation:Some athletes have reported being exploited by sports organizations, further contributing to their challenges.
